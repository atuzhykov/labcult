We are The Laboratory of culture researches

The Laboratory of Cultural Research was founded in 2016 in Chernivtsi. The idea of creation was based on the formalization of projects of cultural activists and obtaining legal status for more institutional activity. The organization works in the sphere of cultural and educational management, and also deals with various cultural practices, researches and experiments, interregional and international collaborations.

About us

Our aim is to experiment with a variety of cultural practices, to create a platform not only for artistic events, but also for artistic dialogue and discussions and informal communication. We want to promote the consolidate cultural potential of Bukovina and to synthesize high quality culture product.

The main areas with which we work: art, culture, informal education, civil society, media.
